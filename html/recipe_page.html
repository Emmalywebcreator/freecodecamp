How to Make Tiger Nut Drink

Tiger nut drink, also known as Kunu Aya, is a refreshing and nutritious beverage made from soaked tiger nuts, dates, and sometimes coconut. Itâ€™s naturally sweet, lactose-free, and packed with fiber and vitamins.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of tiger nuts (soaked overnight)
  • 1 cup of coconut chunks (optional)
  • 6 dates (for sweetness)
  • 3 cups of water (for blending)
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak the tiger nuts overnight to soften them.
  2. Remove the seeds from the dates.
  3. Blend the soaked tiger nuts, coconut, dates, and ginger with water until smooth.
  4. Use a fine sieve or cheesecloth to strain the mixture.
  5. Pour into a clean bottle and chill in the refrigerator before serving.
